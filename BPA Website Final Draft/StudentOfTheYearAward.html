Student of the Year Award

Purpose

To recognize those students who have made significant contributions to Business Professionals of America as a student member.

Eligibility

Registered, student members in good standing with Business Professional of America. Current or former National Officers will not be considered for this award. To be eligible for the Student of the Year award the applicant must meet all of the following criteria:

  1. Completed a minimum of one full year as a student member
  2. Served as a local, regional or state officer
  3. Attended a minimum of one (1) State Leadership Conference and one (1) National Leadership Conference (Required for Secondary)
  4. Completed in the WSAP
  5. Earned the Ambassador Torch Award
  6. Participated in the BPA Cares Program

The applicant must also meet a minimum of five (5) of the following criteria:

  1. Attended a minimum of one (1) State Leadership Conferences and/or one (1) National Leadership Conference (Optional for Post-Secondary)
  2. Earned the National Merit Scholar Award
  3. Participated in the Intern Program at the National Leadership Conference
  4. Particpated in the Leadership Academy at the National Leadership Conference
  5. Made significant contributions to Special Olympics
  6. Earned the President's Volunteer Service Award
  7. Completed the Student Member Certification Series
  8. Earned the BPA Cares Recruiter of the Year Award
  9. Completed the Life Skills Achievement Program

Lastly, the applicant must submit the following information:

  1. Application Form (Below)
  2. Narrative review of applicant's supportive contributions to BPA on the local, regional, state or national level
  3. Two letters of support for the applicant
  4. One letter must be from a local advisor
  5. One letter must be from the state advisor
  6. Resume for the applicant

Applications Guidelines

All applications must be submitted on the official applcations form. Applications must be submitted by a current active student member. Applications due March 1. Incomplete applications will not be considered. Applications must be submitted at http:/www.bpa.org/submit.

General Information

NO E-MAIL OR MAIL APPLICATIONS WILL BE ACCEPTED. All materials submitted become the property of Business Professionals of America and may be used as the National Center deems appropriate.
It is not necessary for the individual to attend the National Leadership Conference to participate in the program and receive the award.
DO NOT SEND ANY AWARD APPLICATION(S) AS PART OF ANOTHER APPLICATION. AWARD MAY NOT BE SUBMITTED WITH TECHNICAL PROJEXTS OR COMPETITIVE EVENTS. EACH AWARD MUST BE SUBMITTED SEPARATELY.

Recognition

A maximum of one (1) individual from the Secondary and one (1) individual from the Post- Secondary Division will be recognized with a plaque one stage at the National Leadership Conference.
